IQAC Mission

Promoting continuous quality enhancement and institutional excellence through effective processes, data and participation.

🌟 Vision

To embed a sustainable culture of quality that promotes academic excellence, research vitality and administrative efficiency.

🎯 Core Objectives

  • Design and implement internal quality systems.
  • Promote learner-centric pedagogies and assessment.
  • Collect & analyse stakeholder feedback for improvement.
  • Document best practices & support accreditation readiness.
  • Organize capacity-building & quality workshops.

⚙️ Functions & Strategies

  • Coordinate quality initiatives across departments.
  • Collect, manage and interpret institutional data.
  • Prepare AQAR and other accreditation documents.
  • Conduct audits, workshops and stakeholder consultations.
  • Monitor action plans and completion rates.
